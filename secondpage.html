About Me

I love to cook! I am in the process of learning how to make a variety of different indian meals right now!

Here is what I have learned how to make so far

  • Paneer Tikka
  • Pav Bhaji
  • Dosa

Lately, I’ve been learning how to cook Indian food. It’s been a mix of fun and a bit challenging, but I’m slowly getting the hang of it. I started with some basic recipes like dal, chole, and paneer curry. It’s interesting how a few simple spices can make such a huge difference in flavor. At first, I thought it was just about following the recipe, but I quickly realized there’s a lot more to it. Timing is important, and so is understanding how each spice works. I’ve had a few failed attempts, like burning the onions or making the curry too salty, but it’s all part of the process. I’m still figuring out how to make the perfect roti; sometimes they turn out great, and other times they’re just weirdly shaped. But I’m enjoying the learning process, and it’s a nice break from studying. Plus, it’s pretty satisfying when I get a dish right and it actually tastes good. Overall, cooking Indian food has been a fun way to try new things, and I’m excited to keep experimenting and learning more.
